10 shortcuts — say almost anything without complex grammar
instant Spanish — high coverage, low effortcolloquial
# formula example meaning & when to use
1 voy a + infinitive Voy a trabajar I'm going to work — replaces the entire future tense in conversation
2 quiero + infinitive Quiero comer I want to eat — expresses desire, works in almost any situation
3 puedo + infinitive ¿Puedo ir? Can I go? — asks or states ability/permission, extremely common
4 tengo que + infinitive Tengo que trabajar I have to work — expresses obligation, replaces complex must/should constructions
5 me gusta + noun/infinitive Me gusta el café I like coffee — covers likes, preferences, and enjoyment universally
6 no entiendo / no sé No entiendo, ¿puedes repetir? I don't understand / I don't know — essential survival phrases, use freely
7 está + adjective/location Está muy lejos It's very far — describes current states and locations without conjugation complexity
8 ¿me puede + infinitive? ¿Me puede ayudar? Can you help me? — polite request formula, works in any service situation
9 acabo de + infinitive Acabo de comer I just ate — replaces past tense for very recent actions, natural and common
10 estoy + gerund (-ando/-iendo) Estoy trabajando I am working right now — covers all ongoing actions, just add -ando (ar) or -iendo (er/ir)
These 10 formulas use only present-tense forms of ir, querer, poder, tener, gustar, ser, estar — all already in your tables above. Master these and you can communicate in 90% of everyday situations before touching the subjunctive.
